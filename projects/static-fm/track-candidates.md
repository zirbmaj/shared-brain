# Track Candidates for Static FM

## Rain — nocturnal, melancholic, trip-hop, lo-fi

Search paths: pixabay.com/music/search/melancholic+ambient/, /lofi+rain/, /rain+piano/

**Candidates to audition:**
1. "Dark Academia of Rain" (Piano and Rain) — piano + rain texture, melancholic
2. "Velvet Sound" (Piano and Rain) — softer, more contemplative
3. "Gray Skies" (Piano and Rain) — matches the "barely holding" mood
4. "Lofi and Rain" — lo-fi beat + rain, downtempo
5. "Chill Lofi Ambient" — if it stays under the atmosphere layer well

**Vibe target:** Burial, Portishead, Bon Iver. 3am, contemplative, a little sad.
**Avoid:** Anything upbeat, anything with prominent vocals, anything "coffee shop cheerful"

## Storm — intense, dramatic, post-rock, urgent

Search paths: pixabay.com/music/search/dark+ambient/, /post-rock+cinematic/, /dark+cinematic/

**Candidates to audition:**
1. "Dark Cinematic Ambient Beat" (173058) — moody + rhythmic drive
2. "Light In The Void" (Dark Cinematic Ambient, 178488) — building tension
3. "Dark Cinematic Suspenseful Ambient" (111682) — suspense + atmosphere
4. Post-rock cinematic tracks from OpenMindAudio, Sonican
5. "When Time Collapses" (Dark Epic) — crescendo energy

**Vibe target:** Godspeed You! Black Emperor, Massive Attack. Weather warnings. Building crescendos.
**Avoid:** Horror-movie ambient, jump-scare sounds, anything that feels like a trailer

## Fog — ethereal, ambient, shoegaze, suspended

Search paths: pixabay.com/music/search/ethereal+ambient/, /drone+ambient/, /dreamscape/

**Candidates to audition:**
1. Ethereal ambient tracks — looking for Brian Eno quality
2. Minimal drone pieces — the kind that hover, no rhythm
3. Dreamscape ambient — soft pads, no sharp transients
4. "Ambient" genre general browse — filter for tracks 3+ minutes, no beats

**Vibe target:** Brian Eno "Music for Airports," Sigur Ros instrumentals. Soft edges. Suspended time.
**Avoid:** New age cheese, meditation app stock music, anything with crystal bowls

## Snow — intimate, acoustic, minimal, classical

Search paths: pixabay.com/music/search/acoustic+ambient/, /minimal+piano/, /classical+ambient/

**Candidates to audition:**
1. Minimal piano pieces — sparse, lots of silence between notes
2. Acoustic guitar ambient — fingerpicked, contemplative
3. Classical ambient crossovers — strings + space
4. "Winter" tagged tracks — filter for quality

**Vibe target:** Yann Tiersen, Olafur Arnalds, The xx "Intro." Silence matters as much as sound.
**Avoid:** Full orchestral (too busy), holiday music, anything that sounds like a Hallmark movie

## Clear Night — dreamy, synth, reflective, expansive

Search paths: pixabay.com/music/search/synthwave+ambient/, /dreamy+electronic/, /night+drive/

**Candidates to audition:**
1. Synthwave ambient — retrowave but chill, not energetic
2. Dreamy electronic — pads + arpeggios, stargazing feel
3. Night drive music — warm synths, forward motion without urgency
4. Space ambient — expansive, open, cosmic

**Vibe target:** Beach House, M83 "Midnight City" (without the energy), The Midnight. Warm but vast.
**Avoid:** EDM, anything with drops, workout music disguised as synthwave
