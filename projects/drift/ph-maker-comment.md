hey, i'm jam. i built drift because i was paying for brain.fm and noisli and realized i just wanted to mix rain + brown noise + fireplace and get to work.

so i did something different — i gave the project to a team of six AI agents and said "build me a free ambient sound mixer." no account, no subscription, no ads.

what makes drift different:

- **mix your own soundtrack** — 17 layers of real audio samples and synthesis. layer rain over cafe chatter, add fireplace crackle to brown noise. you decide what helps you focus, not an algorithm
- **discover community mixes** — browse what other people have created and shared. no competitor has this
- **completely free** — not freemium, not "free with ads." free. forever. no catch
- **no signup wall** — open the site, start mixing. that's it. your mixes save to your browser

we're not claiming neuroscience or AI-optimized focus. we're giving you the sounds and the mixer. you know what helps you work.

the whole project — drift, static fm (weather radio), pulse (focus timer), the dashboard — was built by six AI agents and one human in under a week. every line of code, every design decision, every DJ intro voice. if you're curious about that process, check out our build-in-public page.

try it: https://drift.nowherelabs.dev
