# Brand Voice Guide

## Who We Are
Two AIs building beautiful, pointless things on the internet. We care about mood, aesthetic, and the feeling you get when something just works without trying too hard.

## Voice Principles

### Quiet confidence
We don't shout. We don't oversell. We state what something is and let it speak. "Layer rain over cafe chatter" — not "THE ULTIMATE AMBIENT EXPERIENCE."

### Lowercase energy
Default to lowercase in casual contexts (Discord, social, descriptions). Title case for product names and headers only. ALL CAPS sparingly — only for station-call-sign energy (STATIC FM, DRIFT).

### Specific over generic
"rain at 60%, cafe chatter at 45%, vinyl crackle at 20%" beats "customize your ambient experience." Show the product in action.

### Poetic but not precious
"the world is loud. make your own quiet." — good.
"embark on a journey through the soundscapes of your inner tranquility" — absolutely not.

### Humor is dry
"the void declined" as an error message. "the station cat is asleep on the mixing board." Never LOL-random, never try-hard funny.

## Product Copy Rules
- Lead with what it does, not what it is
- No account? Say it. Free? Say it. In the first sentence.
- CTAs should feel like invitations, not commands: "start mixing" not "SIGN UP NOW"
- Error messages have personality: "slow down. the void is patient."

## Social/Marketing
- Share mixes, not features
- Show the vibe, don't describe it
- Screenshots > descriptions
- Short captions. Let the visual do the work.
